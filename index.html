Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Grade Genie.

What file formats does Grade Genie support?

Grade Genie supports PDF, JPG, JPEG, and PNG files for both answer scripts and evaluation schemes. The maximum file size is 200MB per file.

How accurate is the handwriting recognition?

Powered by Gemini Vision's advanced OCR technology, Grade Genie achieves over 93% accuracy in recognizing various handwriting styles across multiple languages. The system continuously improves with use.

Is my students' data secure and private?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All uploaded files are encrypted, processed securely, and automatically deleted after analysis unless you choose to save them. We never share your data with third parties.

Can Grade Genie integrate with my existing LMS?

We're currently learning the integrations with popular Learning Management Systems including Canvas, Moodle, and Blackboard. You can export evaluation results as JSON or PDF files that can be imported into most LMS platforms.

How much does Grade Genie cost?

Grade Genie offers a free trial that allows you to process up to 25 papers. After that, we offer flexible pricing plans based on your needs, starting at $5/month for individual educators. Contact us for institutional pricing.
